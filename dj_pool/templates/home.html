Getting started

The Chicago DJ Pool is an app designed to let you share your music with other DJ's in the area. To get started, head over to the
'Register' page to sign up! You can then use your credentials to log in and view the blog and song pool!

Adding content

After logging in, you can click the "Create" button on both the Blog and Song Pool tabs. Then just fill out the form and your
contribution will be posted onto the database.

Download links

When adding a new song, please include a direct download link in the "Download link" field. This makes it easier for other users to get your tracks quickly!

Your Profile

From your profile, you can see all of your contributions to the site. Here, you also have the option to edit or delete any of your blog posts or songs.
